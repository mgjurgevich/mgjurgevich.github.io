Kickstarter Subscriptions

Home/Portfolio/Kickstarter

Kickstarter, a crowdfunding site currently allows projects to be backed once. Projects aren’t funded until a goal is met.

Our job was to add a new feature to support projects on Kickstarter that require recurring payments.

Overview:

3 designer project for General Assembly UXDI

Role:

User Research, Competitive Analysis, Sketching, User Flows, Prototypes, Usability Testing, Project Management

Timeframe:

9 days

Tools:

Pen & Graph Paper, Sketch, InVision

The Challenge

Expand the types of projects on Kickstarter to include ones that require recurring payments.

Design a new feature for users to create projects that require recurring payments and a section for backers to discover these projects.

The Solution

Subscription based projects.

Research

In the Kickstarter world, there are two types of users:

Entrepreneurs & Backers

We interviewed and surveyed 17 entrepreneurs and 26 backers. Based on our user research, we found that both backers and entrepreneurs liked the Kickstarter interface and found it easy to use.

Entrepreneurs

The process of setting up a project is easy, it’s reputable, and reaches a wide audience.

“It was successful, it was easy to set up, and we got a lot of eyes on our work without much effort."

“I’ve used Kickstarter for its credibility; there are standards you have to meet.”

Backers

Kickstarter is a place to find and support innovative products.

“The idea of crowdfunding a project is an interesting consumer activity--you don't just go online or to the store to buy something.”

"Kickstarter is great because I can support new projects I trust."

Backer

When asked about using recurring payments on Kickstarter, the business models for our new feature were initially confusing to both user groups. Further user research led us to come up with a way to make it easier to understand and create a solution focused on one product: subcriptions

Entrepreneurs

"The first Kickstarter I did I asked everyone I knew and just barely got funded. In the following campaigns I've had repeat backers, which made things much easier."

"Consistent revenue is a beautiful thing, which is why there are so many subscription services."

Backers

"I'd support a project repeatedly if it provided me some kind of value that would fulfill a need or desire I have."

"Any product you need regularly replenished, that's low cost and disposable, works well for a subscription service."
