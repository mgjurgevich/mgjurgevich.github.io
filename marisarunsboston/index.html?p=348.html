Snowy Thursday

March 7, 2013 · by Marisa · in Healthworks Fitness Centers for Women, Weather


Just when nearly all traces of Nemo had disappeared, I was more than disappointed to put on my snow boots this morning. After living in New England for 4 years, I’ve come to dread the month of February, which, after undergoing up to 4 months of rain, wind, freeze, and snow, always feels like the longest, coldest, and most miserable month of the year. When it snows in March, it’s like winter is squeezing lemon on the wounds it inflicted December-February. Dramatic, yes, but from the sad looks on my fellow commuters faces this morning, it’s not that far from true.

Although it may not sound like it, I think I’ve handled the cold pretty well this year. But after hours of running in below freezing temperatures, snow, wind, and through ankle deep puddles, not to mention dealing with all of the above on a daily basis when I’m not running, I’ve had enough. I’ll manage on the short, leisurely run I planned on from work to Marathon Strong training at Healthworks tonight, it’s the 19 miles of slippery sidewalks and roads I’ll be navigating on Saturday that I’m (even more) nervous about.
