training & fundraising for the Boston Marathon 2013

About Marisa Gjurgevich

Marisa is training for the 2013 Boston Marathon on the Healthworks Community Fitness charity team. In between a lot of long and short runs, she’ll also be fundraising $5,000. Here she’ll write about the joys and challenges of training for her very first marathon, new exercise and nutrition habits, raising money, and why she’s proud to be running on the Healthworks Community Fitness Team, in support of an organization that means so much to her and so much to the women and children it helps lead healthier lives.
