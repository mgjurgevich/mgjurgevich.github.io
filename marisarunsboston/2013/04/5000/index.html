$5,000

April 2, 2013 · by Marisa · in Fundraising, Marathon team

I’m thrilled that I met my $5,000 fundraising goal this week! With 13 days to go, I’m eager to continue raising even more vital funds for Healthworks Community Fitness and reach the marathon team’s overall goal of $30,000.

Receiving a donation really is a huge boost of motivation and I’m incredibly grateful to everyone that’s taken the time to contribute and express their support. The dollars raised by the marathon team will be a very significant portion of the total donations that HCF receives this year, which is what makes being part of the John Hancock Non-Profit Marathon Program such a big deal.

It also means that each donation has significant impact on HCF’s two Dorchester community fitness centers and numerous programs for the health and well-being of women and children in need.

As an advisory board member, I’m hopeful that this will be the first of many years in which HCF is granted numbers–I know I certainly couldn’t imagine a better organization to run and fundraise for!
