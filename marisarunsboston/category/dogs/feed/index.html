The Fresh Pond Reservoir

Today I ran 5.5 miles, the furthest I’ve run in years. I’ve had the idea to run the route I ran today in the back of my mind for awhile, but without a real reason to go further than my normal 20-30 minutes, I haven’t.

Shortly after I moved to Cambridge, I found out that the path around the Fresh Pond reservoir covered about 3 miles. Getting there from my apartment is just over a mile, so it always sounded like the ideal 5-mile run. I’m in the area often–grocery shopping nearby almost weekly, but I had never stepped foot on the path until today.

Running Fresh Pond feels like a big feat, after all this time thinking about it, I’m glad I finally made it there. It’s a lovely loop that I plan to take multiple laps around in the coming weeks. There are number of offshooting trails to mix up the scenery, and, more importantly, plenty of cute dogs to keep me entertained for hours.
