About

I’m an experienced fundraiser, project manager, and community builder. I’ve helped develop and implement programs, fostered networks of volunteers, members, students and alumni, and raised vital funds at organizations such as the American Red Cross, Simmons College, and WGBH.

My passion for understanding why nonprofit supporters give—their time, dollars, and ideas—to an organization, and providing a seamless and fulfilling way to do so, led me to pursue User Experience Design. I’m interested in applying what I’ve learned about philanthropy to help solve problems and contribute to innovation that makes positive change.
